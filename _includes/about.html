Who are we?

We are Augsburg Computer Science Students. We endeavor to be leaders in our department. We strive to expand the knowledge of all ACM Members through educational presentations and events. We are determined to advance Computer Science in education and profession by breaking down barriers to entry. We cater to a diverse set of learning styles. We aim to make Computer Science a more social department and promote networking between students, alumni, and industry professionals. We are ACM Chapter 152382.

What is our objective?

  • Learning and teaching new concepts and material outside of the core CS cirriculum
  • Preparing students for the future/success in industry or academia
  • Promoting the exchange of knowledge between students
  • Working with faculty to innovate education
